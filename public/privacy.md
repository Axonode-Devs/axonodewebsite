# Axonode: Privacy Policy

*Last Updated:* [30.06.2026]

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Axonode ("we," "us," "the Organization") collects, uses, stores, and protects information from applicants, members, and visitors to our website. By applying to Axonode, joining as a Member, or using our website, you agree to the practices described here.

## 2. Who Can Use Axonode

Axonode is open to individuals aged 15 and older, primarily high school and university students. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 15. If we become aware that someone under 15 has submitted an application, we will delete the associated data.

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide at Application

When you apply to Axonode through the application form on our website, we collect:

- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number and/or other communication handles (e.g. Instagram)
- Area of interest (Technology, Design, Business & Strategy, Creative Arts)
- Self assessed skill level (Junior, Mid, Senior)
- English proficiency
- Motivation letter, where relevant to the application

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our homepage, we use *Google Analytics* to collect standard usage data such as page views, session duration, device/browser type, and general location. This helps us understand how visitors interact with our site.

### 3.3 Project Related Information

Members who develop projects within Axonode generate project data as described in our Terms of Service and Code of Conduct, including project content, history, and internal discussion related to the project. We may, in the future, collect *usage analytics* (such as page views and time spent) on websites or products built through Axonode projects. This Policy will be updated before any such analytics are implemented.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

- Evaluate applications and determine fit for Axonode
- Communicate with applicants and members
- Organize members into projects based on skill, interest, and experience level
- Operate, maintain, and improve the Axonode website
- Understand visitor behavior through aggregate analytics
- Enforce our Terms of Service and Code of Conduct, including reviewing relevant communications or project data during a dispute or conduct investigation, as authorized by the Board

## 5. Third Party Services

We work with the following third party service providers, who process certain data on our behalf:

- *Resend:* used to send application confirmations, communications, and other organizational emails
- *Google Analytics:* used to collect anonymized/aggregate website usage data on our homepage

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies and applicable data protection standards. *We do not sell member or applicant data to third parties.*

## 6. Data Retention

- *Application data* is retained for as long as necessary to evaluate your application and, if accepted, for the duration of your membership.
- *Project data* is retained in accordance with our Terms of Service, including the "No Takedown" rule. Because Axonode preserves the history and integrity of the community's work, project data and history may remain archived even after a member leaves the Organization. This does not extend to personal contact information beyond what's necessary for organizational records.
- *Analytics data* collected via Google Analytics is retained according to Google's standard retention settings.

## 7. Confidentiality of Internal Discussions

İnternal Axonode discussions and project details are confidential by default unless a project is marked "Public" by the Board. We do not disclose member communications or project information outside the Organization except where required to enforce our Code of Conduct, resolve disputes, or comply with legal obligations.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Note that, per our Terms of Service, the "No Takedown" rule applies to project data and organizational history. Leaving Axonode does not entitle a member to demand removal of project records. Requests regarding personal contact information (e.g. email, phone number) outside of project history can be directed to the Board.

## 9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the personal information we collect from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Axonode grows and our practices evolve, including as new features like project usage analytics are introduced. Material changes will be communicated to members where appropriate. Continued use of Axonode after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

## 11. Governing Law

Axonode is a Türkiye based Organization. This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Türkiye. Members and applicants are responsible for ensuring their participation complies with their own local regulations.
